Our mission

We’re on a mission to give teachers a universal dashboard that lets you understand in real-time how students in your class are progressing. The result is our Zzish Learning Hub, a universal teacher dashboard that any mobile or web education application can plug into to give you real-time data in a consistent and user friendly interface.

We’ve also built Quizalize, a fun and engaging classroom web application you can play on tablets, laptops or desktop computers with your whole class. Instantly create a custom quiz and then play a real-time quiz game with your class. You can use the Zzish Learning Hub to display a live class leaderboard on your electronic whiteboard and then capture results in the class progress dashboard.

How you can help

  1. Create a 10 question quiz in Quizalize to test your class knowledge of a topic you are teaching. (5 minutes)

  2. Run that quiz with your class for real in the classroom. You will need an electronic whiteboard to display the live dashboard and your students will need tablets, laptops or desktop computers to play. (10 minutes)

  3. Send us your feedback on how to make the products more useful to you by filling in our Google Survey (5 minutes).

It’s super simple and in total it should take you 20 minutes to complete all three steps. What’s more your students will love you for introducing a new and engaging way to test their knowledge! And, as a big thank you, we’ll send you a $50/£30 for your help.

Quizalize and the Zzish Learning Hub are completely free!
